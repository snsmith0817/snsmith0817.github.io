MESSAGE TO THE SUPREME ONES

Gracious gods! We call to you now, here, from our mortal realm Earth! We plead to you, free us from our worldly bonds! Offer us ascension to witness your vision! Offer us the gift of Creation! Offer us to remake our own existence! To remake our own meaning of space! Redefine time! Join you among the stars. We make a humble plea to your benevolent nature to grant us this ascension, to permit us to carry your Voice!

We thank you, the all-knowing.
